  • Bermuda Bomber

  • Electro-smog is the invisible electromagnetic radiation emitted from both wireless electronic devices and mains electricity. It can disturb cell communication of living organisms, especially in children.

    The objective is to build early awareness to the existence of electro-smog through play, so that a lifetime of exposure to electro-smog may be reduced as much as possible by subtle changes in behavior.

    The Bermuda Bomber, allows a child to detect and plot the dangerous areas of electro-smog in the living area. When high levels of electro- smog pollution are detected, the propeller of the interactive toy stops spinning and an audible warning sound (nose diving plane) is emitted.

    By creating an early awareness in children of these invisible forces, we can equip them with the knowledge to be safe in the future ever-expanding wireless world.
