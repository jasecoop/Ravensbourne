  • Buc Beats

  • Buc Beats is a musical toy that caters for children of all abilities. It has been designed to captivate, develop and foster an awareness of music in children. The product develops with the child introducing new themes, sounds and skills.

    RFID tags are placed into the vessel, thereby create musical feedback. Using looping, the child can overlap the tags, creating an editable medley of sound and harmony.

    ‘Buc Beats’ is fun, engaging, instant and develops a basic knowledge of harmony and music without the user needing the skills to play an instrument.

    The invisible, seamless integration of the technology gives the product a magical nature. By using the product a child gains insights into sound creation and is able to experiment with music in a more intuitive way.
