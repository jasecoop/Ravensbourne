  • "I pack.”
  • Under The Regulation

  • UK Government announced that cigarette display will be banned in shops. The regulations will come into force for large stores on April 2012 and on April 2015 for all other small shops. The Government said it is keeping an ‘open mind’ on plain packaging for all tobacco products, and is planning a consultation on different opinions before the end of this year. The tobacco industry needs to find a new way to promote their products. Marlboro is chosen as a fictive client for this project.

    This new tobacco pack structure can characterise and differentiate Marlboro brand with its visual appearance and the opening method. The outstanding packaging structure can attract people even if the graphic is taken away from the packaging. In addition, a sticker with the brand logo is inserted between the cover box and the inner box of the packaging. The sticker can expose the brand logo on various objects.
