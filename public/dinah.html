  • Canary

  • Canary is an indoor air pollution monitor which detects the presence of air pollutants within our homes. The product is an adaptation of the relationship between canaries and coal miners.

    Most people are unaware of the importance of good air quality within our homes. The main problem is that our houses are constructed to be well insulated and protected from the elements. So much so that the same air is circulating around our homes due to poor ventilation. The build up of air contaminants could potentially be carcinogenic or fatal to humans.

    Canary acts as a visual and audible cue for the monitoring of indoor air by use of sound pitch and colour semantics. It detects changes in air quality in the ambient air particles using electrochemical sensors these pollutants are measures in particles per million (ppm). The canary will then alert you of the rising levels of air pollution in 3 stages.
