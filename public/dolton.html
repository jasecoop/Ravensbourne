  • Weightless Security

  • A free standing secure public bicycle lock.

    In London, a pop to the shops on your bicycle is often hindered by the worry of theft, carrying a heavy lock is a burden on the joy of cycling and puts many off the idea.

    With this secure cycle lock all that you need worry about is remembering your key. The lock uses RF-ID technology much the same as how the Oyster card and other electronic ID cards work. Due to its simple Design, the installation of the lock can be achieved at an efficient rate.

    Weightless security developed from extensive research and was realised through sketching, CAD, model making and testing. Contact with manufactures was kept throughout to aid the design process, working within their tooling constraints.
