  • Ankle Cooling Brace

  • For the Major project I looked at athletes and what happens when they injure their ankles. Currently there are various bandages, ankle braces, ice packs and cold therapy treatments to buy for different stage of the ankle injury.

    My task was to design an all in one ankle brace.

    There are three parts that make up this ankle brace, part one is the rubber sole to walk with. Part two is the internal polypropylene straps that keep the foot in place. Part three is another brace incorporating cold therapy treatment by a ‘cool-pack’ named MORPHO Cyro-Matrix which stays cold upto 4 hours at a constant temperature and cool 8 hours after.

    The great thing about this ankle brace is that you wouldn’t have to go looking for the bandages, ankle braces, ice packs or even the cold therapy treatments as they are all built in.
