    Ever been in the situation when you had car trouble on a cold day and no warm clothes? Or wanted to have a picnic on one of those rare sunny days but no where to sit?

    This multi- functional blanket is an emergency blanket for those good and bad days and it is always on hand as it is supplied in your Volkswagen car just like the spare wheel kit.

    The blanket is constructed with a fleece side for comfort, a robust space blanket for weatherproofing, which displays the emergency numbers for Europe and a hi-vis edging to increase your visibility to others at night. The rubber straps for the blanket also act as a pair of snow mats for those sinking feeling situations.
