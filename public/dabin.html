  • Cribia

  • My aim was to create a product to encourage real-time play instead of video gaming, as too much is crowding out the kind of play that is essential for young children. Children need to be given the time to use their minds to create wonderful things, with no set scenario. I wanted to bring simplicity back to toys, instead of filling children with evolving technology and being glued to the television.

    Cribia is a toy designed for children. There are no rules or set scenario. This allows the child to create their own game using these simple objects. Cribia is supported by a website. This allows users to upload their ideas and videos to an online community.

    Based on the amount of views their videos receive their contributions climb the leader board and receive gold stars for each 1000 hits.

    This encourages users to share their play as well as introducing a young child to the world of social networking
