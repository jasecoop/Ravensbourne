  • Homegrown

  • HomeGrown is a interactive service that allows people to grow crops on a small scale, experiencing the joys of growing without investing in large bags of soil or expensive equipment.

    HomeGrown allows adults and children to interact with soil allowing them to understand the textures and properties of soil. It is about educating people the importance of soil to ensure they grow and maintain healthy crops.

    The service is aimed at beginners, parents, children and those who are just curious to know more about soil.
