  • Korbie

  • Research highlighted there was a lack of engagement in the modern kitchen by children. This was due to parents lack of involvement, time and cleaning. Korbie allows mess without the abundance of cleaning, fostering issues of care, home cooked food and the fun of baking. Korbie also introduces children to a more animated approach to cooking allowing them to exaggerate the motions and acts of the preparation process including a circular motion to stir and the shaking of Korbie to sieve flour and other ingredients alike.

    When presented with the opportunity, children adore the physical element to the baking process yet are often limited due to the struggle of using a number of utensils together. Korbie has an oversized, steady base and eliminates over-spill often seen with conventional mixing bowls, so as the child need only concentrate on mixing the contained ingredients.
