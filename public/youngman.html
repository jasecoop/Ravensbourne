  • Beginnings, Middles, Ends

  • A brief that aims to instill the love of books and reading into children at a young age; the concept is taken from Alfie (aged 6) who when asked what his favourite thing about reading was, simply replied “The Middle”. The Beginnings are for those who love the characters, The Middles are for those who like it when stuff happens, and The Ends are for those who like it when they all live happily ever after.

    Sometimes it takes a particular something to make books and reading click for a young child. This can be through illustration, a character or the story. This series of twelve Beginnings, Middles and Ends aims to introduce children to the world of books. The books can be mixed and matched giving the child more freedom and choice. The complexity of the language increases across the three stages meaning there is the potential for these to be read as a key stage 1 (ages 4-6) learning aid.
