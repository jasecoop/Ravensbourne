  • White Space

  • A two-part, heavily research-lead project relating to a hardware device encompassing an RGB camera, depth finding camera, headphones, microphone and heads-up display (glasses capable of overlaying information inside a user’s field of view).

    White Space relates to the exploration into the technologies required for immersive experiences where real- world environments are augmented by computer-generated content.

    The project also focuses on the potential applications underpinning such a device be they functional, for entertainment or otherwise.

    The specifics of the hardware and applications can be found at www. ohbee.co.uk/whitespace.
